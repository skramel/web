Wesleyan Vertical Water Tunnel (2016)

Funded by the Army Research Office in collaboration with Cornell University.

Construction of a 14 ft. tall, vertical water tunnel to investigate the dynamics of sedimenting particles. The results of these measurements are to enhance the understanding of fundamental processes appearing in cloud microphysics, optical phenomena and remote sensing. (Google "Sundog" and look at the beautiful consequence of ice crystals in the atmosphere!)
